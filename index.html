How it works

Five steps. One counsellor. From search to seoul.

  1. 01

    Pick a major

    Search 50+ partner universities by program, language of instruction, location, and scholarship availability. We shortlist 3 best-fit options for you.

  2. 02

    Submit documents

    Upload once, apply many. We translate, notarise, and forward to every university on your list.

  3. 03

    Get admitted

    We track every decision and negotiate scholarship offers on your behalf. Average 4–6 weeks to first admission letter.

  4. 04

    Apply for visa

    D-2 visa in 7 days on average. We pre-check every document so the embassy never sends you back.

  5. 05

    Arrive in Korea

    Airport pickup, dorm move-in, sim card, T-money card, and a welcome dinner — all arranged by KUAC. You're not alone on day one.
