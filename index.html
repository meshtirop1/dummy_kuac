How it works

Five steps. One counsellor.
From search to Seoul.

  1. 01

    Pick a major

    Search 50+ partner universities by program, language of instruction, location, and scholarship eligibility. We shortlist the three that fit you best.

  2. 02

    Submit documents

    Upload once, apply many. We translate, notarise, and forward your file to every university on the shortlist.

  3. 03

    Get admitted

    We track every decision and negotiate scholarship offers on your behalf. First admission letter typically lands in 4–6 weeks.

  4. 04

    Apply for visa

    D-2 visa in seven days on average. We pre-check every document so the embassy never sends you back.

  5. 05

    Arrive in Korea

    Met at Incheon, settled into your dorm, and welcomed at dinner. The KUAC counsellor stays with you through orientation week.
